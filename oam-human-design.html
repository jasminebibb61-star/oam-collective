What is Human Design?

Think of it as
the owner's manual
you were never given.

Human Design is a system that synthesizes ancient wisdom and modern science to map out how you're uniquely designed to move through the world. It's based on your birth date, time, and location — and what it reveals can be quietly life-changing.

Most of us spend years trying to operate like everyone around us — and wondering why it feels so hard. Human Design offers a different starting point: what if the way you're wired isn't a problem to fix, but a blueprint to follow?

It reveals

It explains

It reframes

It opens

A new conversation about who you are — one that starts with your design, not someone else's expectations.

The five energy types

Every woman has
a type. What's yours?

Human Design organizes people into five energy types — each with a distinct way of engaging with the world, making decisions, and managing energy. None is better than another. Each is designed differently.

Manifestor ~9% of people

Here to initiate. Built to act independently and create impact. Often misunderstood as too much — when really, she's just ahead.

Generator ~37% of people

Here to respond and build. Has sustainable energy when she's doing work that lights her up — and exhaustion when she's not.

Manifesting Generator ~33% of people

Here to respond, then move fast. Multi-passionate and efficient — often told she does too much, when she's simply wired for more.

Projector ~20% of people

Here to guide. Sees systems and people with unusual clarity. Needs recognition and invitation — not hustle — to thrive.

Reflector ~1% of people

Here to reflect the health of her environment. Rare, sensitive, and deeply wise — often the most misunderstood of all.

Your type is just the starting point. Inside it lives so much more — how you're wired to make decisions, where your energy naturally flows, and the patterns that have quietly shaped your life. That's where it gets personal.
