Signature talks

Three conversations.
One through line.

Each talk stands alone — and together they form a curriculum that takes women from awareness to alignment to intentional design.

Talk One 101

Overload 101 — She's Not Burnt Out. She's Overloaded. There's a Difference.

This talk reframes the conversation around women's exhaustion — moving it from a personal failing to a systemic pattern. Audiences walk away with language they didn't have before and a new understanding of what's actually been driving the cycle.

Talk Two 101

Identity 101 — You Were Never Just One Thing. Reclaiming Yourself Beyond the Role.

What happens when a woman has been defined by her roles for so long that she's lost track of who she is underneath them? This conversation goes there — with honesty, care, and a clear invitation toward something more.

Talk Three 101

Design 101 — Stop Surviving Your Life. Start Designing It.

The permission slip talk. This is where awareness becomes action — where women leave not just inspired, but equipped with a framework for designing what comes next on their own terms.

Corporate & Organizational

Faith & Community Spaces

Women's ministries, community organizations, and faith-based events where the conversation around purpose, identity, and intentional living resonates deeply.
